My Journey After Matric as an Upcoming Programmer

From Engineering Dreams to Coding Realities

My name is Morest, and I’m a 19-year-old who recently completed matric with a strong foundation in math and science. For the longest time, I was captivated by the idea of becoming an engineer, inspired by my surroundings and the environment I grew up in. However, in 2021, my path began to shift. Watching computers on TV sparked a deep curiosity in me, and I became determined to own one. My journey into programming started when I discovered HTML. What began as a simple curiosity turned into a passion.

Early Steps Into Programming

Right after matric, I dove back into coding, relearning and expanding my knowledge. I focused on web development and Python, utilizing free resources on the web and YouTube, as well as taking online courses. It hasn’t been easy—self-learning can be frustrating without guidance, and there were moments I felt like quitting. But I pushed through those challenges, driven by my passion for programming and technology.

Skills and Experience

Today, I have foundational skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. My proudest achievement so far has been creating a mentorship website for a career guidance company that supported me. This project allowed me to apply my skills in a real-world setting, deepening my understanding of web development while contributing to a meaningful cause.

Aspirations and Motivation

My dream is to continue growing as a programmer, solving real-world problems, and contributing to technological advancements. I’m seeking an internship to enhance my skills, gain hands-on experience, and collaborate with experienced professionals. I’m excited about learning from industry experts and making a positive impact.

Overcoming Challenges

Throughout my journey, I faced numerous challenges, from understanding complex programming concepts to balancing my studies with my passion for coding. There were times when I felt overwhelmed, but I learned to break down problems into smaller, manageable tasks. This approach not only helped me tackle coding challenges but also taught me valuable life skills such as perseverance and time management.

Community and Support

One of the most significant factors in my growth has been the support from the programming community. Online forums, coding bootcamps, and local meetups have provided me with a platform to share my experiences, seek advice, and collaborate with like-minded individuals. This sense of community has been instrumental in keeping me motivated and inspired.

Future Goals

Looking ahead, I aim to specialize in full-stack development, mastering both front-end and back-end technologies. I am particularly interested in exploring emerging fields such as artificial intelligence and machine learning. My ultimate goal is to develop innovative solutions that can make a positive impact on society, whether it's through creating accessible educational tools or developing applications that address environmental issues.

Conclusion

My journey from aspiring engineer to budding programmer has been filled with learning, growth, and self-discovery. Each step has brought me closer to my goal of becoming a proficient programmer who can contribute meaningfully to the tech industry. I am excited about the future and the endless possibilities that lie ahead. With determination, hard work, and the support of the community, I am confident that I will achieve my dreams and make a lasting impact in the world of technology.
